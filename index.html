Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Election Protection, a Chrome extension designed to block unwanted political content and provide a cleaner browsing experience. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our extension.

Information We Collect

Blocked Words List and Whitelist

Storage: We store the list of words you choose to block and the websites you add to the whitelist locally on your device using Chrome's storage system. This data is only accessible by the Election Protection extension.

Usage Data

Storage: We collect anonymized usage data to understand how users interact with the extension and to improve its functionality. This data includes information about the features you use and how often you use them.

How We Use Information

Provide and Improve the Extension

We use the blocked words list and whitelist to provide the core functionality of the extension, ensuring that political content is blocked on the sites you visit.

Usage data helps us identify areas for improvement and make necessary updates to enhance the user experience.

Customer Support

If you contact us for support, we may use your information to help resolve any issues you encounter with the extension.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Third-Party Services

We do not share your blocked words list, whitelist, or usage data with any third-party services or partners.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Data Security

We are committed to protecting your data. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard the information we collect and store.

Your Choices

Managing Blocked Words and Whitelist

You can add, remove, and manage your blocked words list and whitelist at any time through the extension's settings.

Uninstalling the Extension

If you no longer wish to use the extension, you can uninstall it from your Chrome browser. Uninstalling the extension will delete all locally stored data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website or through the extension.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at electionblocker@gmail.com.
